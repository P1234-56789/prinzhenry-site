Developer Docs

Live in Hours, Not Weeks

A clean REST API with webhooks, a full sandbox, and SDKs for the most popular platforms.

1
Get your API keys
Sign up for a business account, verify your business registration, and receive your sandbox and production API keys within 24 hours.
2
Create an escrow via API
POST to /v1/transactions with the buyer, seller, amount, and your webhook URL. We return a payment link you embed or send directly.
3
Buyer funds via MoMo or card
Your buyer follows the payment link and pays via MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AT Money, or card — no app download needed.
4
Receive webhooks in real-time
We POST to your webhook_url at every status change — FUNDED, IN_TRANSIT, DELIVERED, COMPLETED, DISPUTED. Update your UI instantly.
5
Release or dispute programmatically
PATCH /transactions/:id/release when the buyer accepts. Or your platform logic can trigger auto-release after your inspection window expires.
