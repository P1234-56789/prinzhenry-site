When Things Go Wrong

How Disputes are Resolved

We freeze the funds and investigate. Evidence is everything — which is why we require both parties to document the transaction.

!
Buyer Raises a Dispute
The buyer has up to 24 hours after delivery to tap "Dispute" and describe the problem — wrong item, damaged, or not received.
Funds Frozen Immediately
📷
Evidence is Collected
Both the buyer and seller upload photos or videos as evidence. The seller's pre-shipment photo (required before dispatch) and the buyer's delivery photo are key.
⚖️
GhEscrow Admin Reviews
Our team reviews all evidence — photos, the in-app chat log, rider confirmation, and GPS data — and makes a ruling within 48 hours.
Human Review
✓
Ruling: Favour Buyer
If the item was not delivered or was clearly misrepresented, the full amount is refunded to the buyer's MoMo wallet.
✓
Ruling: Favour Seller
If the evidence shows the item was delivered in good condition, the funds are released to the seller immediately.
⇄
Auto-Release (No Action)
If the buyer neither accepts nor disputes within 24 hours of confirmed delivery, the funds automatically release to the seller. No one gets stuck waiting forever.

Trust Infrastructure

Why You Can Trust GhEscrow

Every feature is designed to eliminate the people who ruin online commerce in Ghana.

🪪

Ghana Card KYC

Every user must verify their identity with a Ghana Card + live selfie before they can withdraw funds. Anonymous scammers can't operate on our platform.

🚫

Permanent Blacklist

Any user confirmed as a scammer by our admin team is permanently banned. Their Ghana Card number is flagged, preventing re-registration.

⭐

Reputation Score

Both buyers and sellers are rated after every transaction. Your score (out of 5) is public on your profile — making trust visible and earned.

✅

Verified Seller Badge

Complete 5 successful escrow transactions and earn a Verified Seller badge for your profile and social media. A signal that you are not a scammer.

🏛️

Licensed Payment Partners

We integrate with Bank of Ghana-licensed gateways (Paystack / Hubtel) to hold your funds. We never store money in a personal account.

💬

Transaction Chat Log

Every message between buyer and seller is tied to the transaction and preserved as evidence. No more "he said / she said" with no proof.
